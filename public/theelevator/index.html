Tom Petridis Profile
The Elevator
The Elevator is a short virtual experience about the irrational fear of elevators & claustrophobia. Based on my personal experience in a hotel as a child in Crete, the experience takes you through the thoughts and fears that I felt. The means of interaction are limited and gameplay is distinctly lacking, these are key features of the environment as they are representative of the real experiences that claustrophobia creates.
As my first virtual environment, The Elevator relies mostly on sound design and simple animation to present my ideas. The sound design is most important as it elevates the game from a simple environment to a more personal experience and the animation physically projects the feeling of suffocation from a tight space that inspires this project. The Elevator is my experiential way of presenting the fears and imaginations that manifest from claustrophobia.
