Eleventh Planet
Eleventh Planet is a site-specific interactive Augmented Reality (AR) artwork. The AR transforms a chrome sphere public art sculpture in front of Eastern Eleven into a series of 3D animated artworks that present transforming worlds to explore, inspired by the materiality of the cosmos and layers of deep time.
Activate the AR artwork by downloading the App, following the prompts and pointing your smart-device’s camera at the tiled walls under the sphere sculpture.
Location: Eastern Eleven, 11‑23 Eastern Rd, South Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.
