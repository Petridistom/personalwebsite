Eleventh Planet
Eleventh Planet is a site-specific interactive Augmented Reality (AR) artwork. The AR transforms a chrome sphere public art sculpture in front of Eastern Eleven into a series of 3D animated artworks that present transforming worlds to explore, inspired by the materiality of the cosmos and layers of deep time. Activate the AR artwork by downloading the App, following the prompts and pointing your smart-device’s camera at the tiled walls under the sphere sculpture.
Location: Eastern Eleven, 11‑23 Eastern Rd, South Melbourne, Victoria, Australia.

Scaniverse
For this project I was part of a group interning with the main artists John and Joanne. My role in this group was as 3D lead, creating 3D models of spaceships from 2D elevations, and assisting other interns with the 3d scanning and remeshing workflow for digitising paper models.
